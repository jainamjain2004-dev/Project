Frequently asked questions

Is my identity visible when I rate someone?▼
No. All ratings are anonymous by default. The person being rated will see their score and qualitative notes, but never the identity of who submitted them. Your profile will only log that you gave a rating, not to whom.
How does Veritas prevent fake or inflated ratings?▼
Our AI model tracks statistical patterns across all raters. It flags users who give identical scores to everyone, detects coordinated mutual high-rating rings, and cross-references relationship claims against professional history. Suspicious ratings are down-weighted before contributing to a score.
Can I claim and manage my own profile?▼
Yes. Once you verify your identity via work email or LinkedIn, you can claim your profile, set your availability status, add context about your career, and request removal of ratings you believe were submitted in bad faith. We review all disputes manually.
How do recruiters access Veritas data?▼
HR teams and hiring managers at subscribing companies can search profiles by Veritas Score range, dimension strength, industry, city, and availability. Individual rating breakdowns and testimonials are only visible to verified recruiters, not the general public.
Which cities is Veritas currently available in?▼
We are currently in private beta across Mumbai and Bangalore. Hyderabad, Delhi NCR, and Pune are next in the roadmap. Join the waitlist on our homepage to get notified when we expand to your city.
